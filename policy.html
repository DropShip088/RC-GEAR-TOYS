Refund & Replacement Policy

At RcGearToys, we focus on delivering high-performance RC drift machines with strict quality checks. Due to the nature of our products, we follow a replacement-only policy.

1. Replacement eligibility

We offer replacement only under the following conditions:

  • Product is damaged during delivery.
  • Product is defective or not working on arrival.
  • Wrong product received.
  • Issue must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Unboxing video is mandatory as proof.
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging.

2. Refunds policy

Refunds and returns are available only if the product is damaged or the wrong item is received.

  • No refund for change of mind.
  • No refund for minor differences in product appearance.
  • No refund for improper usage or handling.
  • No refund for performance expectations not matching personal preference.

3. Replacement process

  • Once your request is approved, we will arrange a replacement unit.
  • Replacement will be processed within 2-5 working days.
  • Delivery time may vary depending on location.

4. Important notes

  • Open box delivery is available.
  • Our RC cars are performance machines, not basic toys. A slight learning curve is normal.
  • Battery, wear-and-tear parts, and accessories are not covered under replacement unless defective on arrival.
Contact for support To request a replacement, email us at support@rcgeartoys.com By placing an order, you agree to this replacement policy.
